About

I was formerly (2021-2023) a Postdoctoral Researcher at CBMN, and member of the Tokarski group.

Between 2019 and 2021, I was a Postdoctoral Researcher at INRAE, and member of the BIBS platform.

Between 2018 and 2019, I was a Postdoctoral Researcher at LIRIS, University Lumière Lyon 2. This work is part of multidisciplinary project, called Optimum and involves researchers in the fields of Computer Science, Art, Architecture, and Geography. The aim of this work is to propose a cartographic tool allowing to retrieve and cluster pictures according to their content, as opposed to their point of view. See more at [link]

Before that, I was a Temporary Lecturer and Research Assistant (Attaché Temporaire d'Enseignement et de Recherche) at Bordeaux University, France.

I obtained my PhD at Bordeaux University, in the Image et Son research group at the LaBRI, under the supervision of Fabien Baldacci, Anne Vialard and Jean-Philippe Domenger. My PhD was about the characterization of tubular organs (such as bronchi, blood vessels, neurons...) through representations such as orthogonal planes and curve-skeletons.

Previously, I did my Master's internship in Heidelberg University, in the Knop lab where my job was to automatize a microscope through automatic cell detection.
